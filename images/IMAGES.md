## Tips
- Use high-quality, professional photography shot at golden hour where possible.
- Keep file sizes below 300 KB per image (use Squoosh or TinyPNG to compress).
- All `alt` text is already written in the HTML.
- WebP format is preferred for performance; JPG is fine as fallback.
