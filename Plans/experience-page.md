# Borneo Beach Villas - Experience Page Implementation Spec

## Project Overview
**Property:** Borneo Beach Villas & Suites, Karambunai Peninsula, Sabah, Malaysia  
**Page Type:** Experience/Activities Landing Page  
**URL Slug:** `/experiences`  
**Tone:** Barefoot Elegance (Sophisticated yet relaxed, emphasizing privacy and space)  
**Target Audience:** Luxury travelers, couples, families, golf enthusiasts, wellness seekers

## Brand Voice Guidelines
- **Primary Voice:** Serene, evocative, sensory-focused
- **Key Phrases:** "Barefoot elegance," "private sanctuary," "where the horizon meets the soul"
- **Avoid:** Generic hotel language, overly commercial CTAs, crowded/touristy implications
- **Emphasize:** Exclusivity, direct beach access, seamless indoor-outdoor living

### 5. Cultural Immersion
**Layout:** Full-width background image with text overlay cards

**Content:**
- **H2:** Sabah's Living Heritage

**Experiences List:**
1. **Kampung Gipsi Cultural Village** (30 min away)
   - Experience authentic Rungus longhouse architecture and traditions in this living museum.
   
2. **Tip of Borneo (Tanjung Simpang Mengayau)** (1 hour north)
   - Where the South China Sea meets the Sulu Sea. Dramatic cliff-top views, renowned sunset spot, and surfer's paradise.
   
3. **Kota Kinabalu City** (30 min south)
   - Bustling markets (Gaya Street Sunday Market), iconic City Mosque, waterfront seafood restaurants serving Malaysia's freshest catch.

**Visual:** Collage or carousel of cultural imagery (longhouses, Tip of Borneo cliffs, markets)
