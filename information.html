About Us

Borneo Beach Villa & Suite is a privately owned, boutique beachfront retreat situated on the idyllic Karambunai Peninsula — one of Sabah's most scenic and unspoilt coastlines on the north-west shore of Borneo.

Our villas are designed as self-contained private sanctuaries, ideal for families, couples, and groups seeking an authentic yet luxurious coastal experience. The property operates on a self-check-in basis, giving guests complete freedom and privacy throughout their stay.

With direct beach access, panoramic sea views, private pools, and carefully curated interiors, each villa is a home away from home — blending Bornean warmth with modern comfort.

Self Check-In Property. Our villas operate with a self-check-in system for maximum guest privacy and flexibility. Detailed arrival instructions and door codes are sent ahead of your stay.

How to Get Here

Borneo Beach Villa & Suite is located approximately 30 km from Kota Kinabalu International Airport (BKI) — roughly a 30 to 45-minute drive depending on traffic conditions.

From the Airport

  • Metered taxi or ride-hailing services (e.g. Grab) are available at the airport. Estimated fare to the villa:RM50 - RM80
  • Car rental is recommended for guests who wish to explore Kota Kinabalu and surrounding attractions independently. All major rental companies are available at the airport.
  • Private transfers can be arranged upon request — please contact us in advance for assistance.

Driving Directions

From Kota Kinabalu city centre, take the Jalan Tuaran highway northbound. Follow signs towards Karambunai and turn off at Jalan Sepanggar Bay. Click at the button below for directions.

General Information

To ensure the comfort of all guests and the integrity of our beautiful surroundings, we kindly ask all guests to observe the following house rules and guidelines.

House Rules

  • This is a self-check-in property. Access codes and instructions are sent in advance — please do not share your access code with unauthorised persons.
  • No outside visitors are permitted without prior authorisation from management.
  • No pets allowed on the property.
  • Strong-smelling fruits (e.g. durian) are not permitted inside the villas or on the covered terrace areas.
  • Strictly no smoking inside the villas. Designated smoking areas are available outdoors.
  • No parties, events, or large gatherings. The property is designed for private, quiet enjoyment.
  • Please respect the surrounding natural environment and neighbouring properties — keep noise levels low, especially between 10 PM and 8 AM.

Beach & Pool Safety

  • Guests swim at their own risk. No lifeguard is on duty.
  • Children must be supervised by an adult at all times near the pool and beach.
  • Appropriate swimming attire must be worn in pool areas. No street clothes in the pool.
  • Please shower before entering the pool.
  • The beach is accessible via our private beach path — guests are reminded to take care of the natural environment and avoid leaving litter.
  • Sea conditions can change. Please check with our team if you are unsure about swimming conditions on any given day.

Wildlife

  • The Karambunai Peninsula is home to a rich variety of wildlife including monitor lizards, long-tailed macaques, sea eagles, and various reptiles.
  • Do not feed or approach wildlife. Maintain a respectful distance at all times.
  • Secure all food and waste to avoid attracting animals to the villa.
  • Any wildlife concerns or sightings should be reported to our team immediately.

Sustainability. We are committed to protecting Borneo's extraordinary natural heritage. We ask all guests to join us in conserving water and energy, minimising single-use plastics, and treating the surrounding ecosystem with care and respect.
